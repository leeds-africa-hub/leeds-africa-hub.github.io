The Leeds-Africa Conference on Data Science and AI across mathematics and sciences is a venue for sharing insights across research areas that are being transformed by developments in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Data Science. We seek to bring together experts from the UK and across Africa to build new partnerships and collaborations, giving researchers the opportunity to share their research with new communities and discover new applications for theoretical developments in mathematical and computational methods.

Areas of interest include Agriculture, Astronomy, Bioinformatics, Climate Science, Ecology, Financial Mathematics, Fluid Mechanics as well as advances in Computer Science and Mathematics. As well as these topics, we are keen to invite interest in other application areas - please let us know if you would like to present your research in other fields!

Registration for the conference is now closed. If you have any questions or would like to discuss your application, please contact the organisers.

The conference is jointly organised by the University of Pretoria and the University of Leeds, and will take place at the Future Africa Campus in Pretoria, South Africa.

Organizers

The principal organisers are Dr John Ilee (Leeds), Dr Jack Radcliffe (Pretoria) and Dr Richard Mann (Leeds).
