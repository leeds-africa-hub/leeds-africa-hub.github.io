You can register your interest in attending the conference using the application form. Please specify whether you would like to present your work at the conference, and if possible give a description of your intended presentation. If you do not yet have an abstract prepared it is fine to give a brief overview of your research interests.

Please use the form to specify whether you require financial assistance to support your attendance at the conference. We aim to provide financial support to allow researchers to attend at various career stages, and those who do not have existing sources of research funding. A central purpose of the conference is to bring together communities that may not be able to attend other international conferences due to costs and travel constraints.

If you have any questions about registration, or wish to discuss your attendance or financial assistance, please contact the organisers.
