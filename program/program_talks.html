2—6 October 2023
Future Africa, University of Pretoria
Home Registration Program Directions Contact
The conference will consist of three days of plenary talks across a variety of disciplines, with the goal of sharing ideas and approaches across many applications of data science and artificial intelligence. For the final two days, there will be several parallel sessions on more focussed topics (which may include advancing specific research areas or developing techniques) with the aim of developing long term collaborations.
