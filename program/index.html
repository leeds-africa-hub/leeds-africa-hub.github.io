Thursday 5th October 2023 (Hatfield Campus)

09:00 The Unconference Sessions
Everyone
The final two days of the meeting will be made available to participant-led meetings. We will have several rooms to run parallel sessions on any topics relevant to the conference. These may include (but are certainly not limited to) discussion of topics relevant to researchers across multiple disciplines, seminars/tutorials on particular techniques, or 'hackathons' designed to tackle specific questions with a range of new approaches.

We will contact participants over the next several weeks with information on how to propose to lead an unconference session. There will also be capacity to host sessions on topics that arise naturally during the first few days of the meeting.
